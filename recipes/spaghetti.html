Spaghetti Recipe

Recipe Description

This spaghetti recipe embodies the essence of traditional Italian cuisine, intertwining the simplicity of fresh ingredients with a symphony of flavors that dance across the palate. Each strand of pasta is meticulously coated in a luscious, homemade tomato sauce, enriched with a secret blend of herbs and spices, promising a culinary journey that's both comforting and exhilarating.

Ingredients

  • Some Noodles (Preferably Fettucini)
  • Pasta sauce
  • Fresh Mozerella
  • Tomatoes
  • Meatballs

Instructions

  1. Boil water & preheat oven to 400*F
  2. Once water is boiling, insert Noodles & let them cook 7-8mins
  3. Once oven is preheated, insert Meatballs & let them cook 15-20mins flipping halfway
  4. Once noodles & meatballs are done, strain noodles put meatballs in same dish as noodles
  5. Add sauce and cheese (if you want) and stir, then your dish is ready to serve
